Nastooh Taheri Javan, PhD

PostDoc Researcher, Amirkabir University of Technology

What I am all about...

I am an Assistant Professor at Imam Khomeini International University (IKIU). I completed my post-doctoral fellowship at Amirkabir University of Technology (Tehran Polytechnic), where I also earned my PhD and MS in computer engineering. My research interests are in the area of wireless computer networks and network coding theory, covering everything from theoretical concepts to design and implementation. I actively collaborate with researchers in various areas of computer science, especially in resource management and network architecture. I am currently a Senior Member of IEEE (SMIEEE). I joined IKIU as an assistant professor in the department of computer engineering in September 2020.

I am also the co-founder and CEO of Barbod, a knowledge-based holding company focused on hardware design and electronic product development. As the CEO, I oversee all aspects of the business. I have over 10 years of experience in executive management and leading R&D teams in the IT industry, with a strong record in developing solutions for complex technical problems. Before founding Barbod, I served as the CEO of PTMI, a company focused on software solutions.
